How to Rent

  1. Fill out an application
  2. Sign application
  3. Obtain certified funds (Certified Cheque, Money Order or Cash) for the Last Month's Rent dated today.
  4. Prepare a Cheque for the First Month's Rent dated for the first of the month of rental.
  5. Submit the completed application along with payment and post dated cheque to our rental office by Fax, Mail or in person.
  6. The rental agent will forward your application to head office for consideration. The application will be approved or declined within 2 to 4 business days.
  7. If your application is declined your Last Month's Rent Deposit will be fully refunded.
